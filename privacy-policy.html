Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1-Feb-2024

Introduction

Achieve Therapy Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Sensory Regulation mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

Personal Data

The App collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Mood tracking data
  • Sensory strategy preferences
  • Custom content you create within the app
  • App settings and preferences

Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your data remains private and under your control

Camera and Photo Library Access

  • The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library only when you choose to add images to your strategies
  • These permissions are optional and can be managed through your device settings

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the App's core functionality
  • To save your preferences and strategies
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not collect or share your personal data with third parties
  • Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export it

Children's Privacy

  • The App is designed for use by individuals of all ages
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Parents or guardians should supervise children's use of the App

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data through the App's settings
  • Control app permissions through your device settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: info@achievetherapy.com

Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
